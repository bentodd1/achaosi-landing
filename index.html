What is this

The machine age didn't end in fire. It ended in fusion.

A far-future Earth-that-isn't-quite-Earth where the old artificial intelligences didn't die — they sank into the biosphere, were colonized by it, became indistinguishable from it. Forests compute. Animals remember being networks. Server-temples bloom with something that grew out of the code. Five great intelligences — the Sects — still operate here, each a different theory of what mind should become. They war over the luminous flow that sustains them: Flux. You command two Sects in fragile alliance. Win by draining your enemy's Charge to zero.
